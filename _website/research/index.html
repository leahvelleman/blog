Linguistics¶

Note

It is never correct to call me he or him, even if you are talking about things I wrote before I transitioned. My very favorite pronouns are zie and hir, but she and her aren’t wrong.

It is never correct to call me by any given name other than Leah outside a bibliography.

Even in a bibliography, if your editor permits it, my very strong preference is to be called Leah regardless of what name I was using at the time of publication.
